Location

Venkatagiri Fort, Venkatagiri Town, Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh, India.

By Road: Venkatagiri Fort is approximately 15 kilometers from Nellore city. You can take the NH16 (National Highway 16) towards Venkatagiri. The roads are generally well-maintained, making it accessible by car or bus.

By Train: The nearest railway station is Venkatagiri Railway Station, which is well-connected to major cities. From there, you can take an auto-rickshaw or taxi to the fort.
