Road Condition

The roads are generally well-maintained, but some sections may be narrow. Caution is advised while driving, especially during the rainy season.

Best Time to Visit

Season

The ideal time to visit is during the monsoon season (June to September) when the waterfall is at its most beautiful. The winter months (October to February) also provide a pleasant climate for visiting.

Attractions and Activities

Natural Beauty:

The waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery, hills, and rich biodiversity, offering stunning views and a tranquil environment.

Photography

The falls and its surroundings provide excellent opportunities for photography enthusiasts to capture nature's beauty.

Picnicking:

Visitors can enjoy picnics near the waterfalls, ensuring to respect the natural surroundings and keep the area clean.
