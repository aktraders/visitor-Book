By Train

Nearest Railway Station: Tirupati Railway Station Distance: About 15 kilometers from the fort. Connectivity: Tirupati Railway Station is a major railway hub with connections to major cities in India. From the station, you can take a taxi or use local transport to reach Chandragiri Fort.

By Road

From Tirupati: The fort is well-connected by road, and the drive from Tirupati to Chandragiri Fort takes around 30 minutes. Route: You can take NH71 (National Highway 71) and then follow local roads to reach the fort. From Chennai: Approximately 130 kilometers away. You can drive or take a bus from Chennai to Tirupati and then continue to Chandragiri.

Public Transport

Buses: There are state-run and private buses that connect Tirupati to Chandragiri. You can check local bus services for schedules and routes. Taxis and Auto-Rickshaws: Available in Tirupati for direct travel to the fort.
