Location

Mypadu Beach, Mypadu Village, Peddaganjam, Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh, India.

By Road: Mypadu Beach is approximately 20 kilometers from Nellore city center. You can reach it via National Highway 16 (NH16) and then take the local roads toward Mypadu Village. Well-maintained roads make it accessible for cars, bikes, and buses.

By Train: The nearest major railway station is Nellore Railway Station, which is well-connected to various cities in India. From the station, you can hire a taxi or use local transport to reach the beach.
