Location:

Distance from Kadapa City: The temple is situated approximately 3 kilometers from the center of Kadapa town.
Address: The temple is located in the Nagarajupeta area of Kadapa.

By Road: The temple is easily accessible by local transportation like auto-rickshaws and buses from various parts of Kadapa.
Landmarks: It is close to notable locations like the Kadapa Collectorate and the local market, making it easy to find.

The temple is dedicated to Lord Rama and is a popular pilgrimage site, known for its beautiful architecture and serene environment.
