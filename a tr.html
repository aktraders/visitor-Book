Thumpala reservoir


                 

Features:

size:

The reservoir covers a substantial area and has a considerable water storage capacity, which varies based on seasonal rainfall and other factors.

scenic beauty

The surrounding area offers picturesque views, with lush green surroundings and serene waters, making it a pleasant spot for nature enthusiasts.

Recreation

While the primary purpose is irrigation and water supply, the reservoir's tranquil environment can be enjoyable for casual visits and picnics.

Habitat:

The reservoir supports local wildlife and bird species, contributing to the region's biodiversity.

Water Management:

It plays a crucial role in managing water resources for agriculture and helps in maintaining the ecological balance in the area.

is accessible via road networks that connect it to nearby towns and cities within the district. Local transportation options include buses and private vehicles.
