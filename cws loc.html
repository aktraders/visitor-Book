Location:

District: East Godavari, State: Andhra Pradesh, Nearest Town: Kakinada (approximately 15 kilometers away)

Nearest Railway Station: Kakinada Junction, which is well connected to major cities like Chennai, Hyderabad, and Vijayawada. From Kakinada Junction, you can travel by taxi or local transport to Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary.

From Kakinada: Take NH 16 towards Rajahmundry and then follow local roads to Coringa. The sanctuary is well signposted from Kakinada. From Rajahmundry: Drive towards Kakinada on NH 16, and then take the local roads to Coringa.
