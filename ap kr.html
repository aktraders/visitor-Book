Purpose and Importance:

Irrigation:

The reservoir plays a vital role in providing water for agriculture in the otherwise arid region of Anantapur. It supports the cultivation of crops and helps sustain local farming communities.

Flood Management:

By capturing excess rainwater during the monsoon season, the reservoir helps in controlling floods and preventing waterlogging in the surrounding areas.

Ecological Benefits:

The reservoir contributes to local biodiversity, providing habitat for various bird species and supporting the regional ecosystem.

Visiting Information:

By Road:

Accessible from Anantapur, approximately 40-50 kilometers away. The route is generally straightforward, with local roads connecting the reservoir to Anantapur and nearby towns.

By Public Transport:

Local buses and taxis can be used to reach the reservoir from Anantapur and surrounding areas.

activities

Scenic Views:

The reservoir offers picturesque views of the water body against the backdrop of the surrounding landscape. It is a peaceful spot for relaxation and appreciating natural beauty.

Bird Watching:

The reservoir attracts various bird species, making it a good location for bird-watching enthusiasts. The area around the reservoir provides opportunities to observe both migratory and resident birds.

Photography:

The tranquil environment and scenic views offer excellent opportunities for photography, especially during early morning or late afternoon when the lighting is optimal.
