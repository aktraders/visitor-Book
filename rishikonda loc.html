Location: Rishikonda Beach
District: Visakhapatnam
State: Andhra Pradesh

Nearest Town: Visakhapatnam City (approximately 8 kilometers away)
Nearest Railway Station: Visakhapatnam Railway Station, which is well connected to major cities like Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kolkata. From the railway station, you can take a taxi or local transport to reach Rishikonda Beach.

From Visakhapatnam City To Rishikonda Beach: Take Beach Road and follow the signs towards Rishikonda. The beach is about a 20-minute drive from the city center.
Local Travel: Auto-rickshaws, taxis, and local buses are available for convenient transport from various parts of the city to Rishikonda Beach, which is known for its scenic beauty and water sports.
