Location:

Araku Valley is situated about 120 kilometers from Visakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh.

altitude

It is located at an elevation of around 1,300 meters (4,300 feet) above sea level.

climate

The climate is generally mild and pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C throughout the year. It has a temperate climate compared to the hot plains of Andhra Pradesh.
