Location:

Distance from Kadapa: Approximately 90 kilometers (around 2 to 3 hours by road).
Nearest Major Town: The hills are closer to Tirupati, which is about 30 kilometers from the hills.

By Road: From Kadapa, you can take the NH 40 or local roads towards Tirupati. The drive offers beautiful scenery as you approach the hills.
Public Transport: Buses from Kadapa to Tirupati frequently run, and from Tirupati, you can take local transport to reach the Seshachalam Hills.

Seshachalam Hills are located in the kadapa district, near the town of Tirupati, but they are relatively close to Kadapa.
