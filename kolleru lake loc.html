Kolleru Lake Location
District: West Godavari
State: Andhra Pradesh

Nearest Town: Kolleru (approximately 5 kilometers away)
Nearest Railway Station: Kaikaluru Railway Station, about 12 kilometers from the lake.
From Kaikaluru Railway Station to Kolluru Lake:
Travel by road via local buses or taxis. It takes approximately 20-30 minutes to reach the lake.

Local Travel: Auto-rickshaws and taxis are available for transport from Kolleru and nearby towns. Kolluru Lake is known for its rich biodiversity and beautiful natural surroundings, making it a popular spot for bird watching and photography.
