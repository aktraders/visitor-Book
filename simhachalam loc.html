Location:Simhachalam
District: Visakhapatnam
State: Andhra Pradesh

Nearest Town: Visakhapatnam City (approximately 16 kilometers away)
Nearest Railway Station: Visakhapatnam Railway Station, which is well connected to major cities like Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kolkata. From the railway station, you can take a taxi or local transport to reach Simhachalam.

From Visakhapatnam City To Simhachalam Temple:Take NH 16 towards the north and follow signs for Simhachalam. The temple is approximately 16 km from the city center.
Local Travel: Auto-rickshaws and taxis are readily available for the journey from Visakhapatnam City to Simhachalam. The area is well-signposted, making it easy to navigate.
