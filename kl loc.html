Location:

Eluru: Located about 15 kilometers southwest of Kolleru Lake. Eluru is a major town in the West Godavari district and serves as a key access point for visiting the lake.

Vijayawada: Approximately 60 kilometers east of Kolleru Lake. Vijayawada is a major city in the Krishna district and provides access via NH16.

Rajahmundry: About 90 kilometers north of Kolleru Lake. Rajahmundry is another significant city in Andhra Pradesh with transport connections to the lake.
