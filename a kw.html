kondapalli waterfalls


                 

Description

Kondapalem Waterfalls is nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Eastern Ghats. The waterfall is relatively small compared to other major waterfalls in India but offers a serene and tranquil environment. It cascades down from a moderate height, creating a picturesque setting ideal for nature enthusiasts and photographers.

Accessibility

Transport:

by road

The waterfall is accessible by road from major towns and cities in Andhra Pradesh. The nearest major city is Ongole, which is about 50 kilometers away. From Ongole, you can take a local bus or hire a cab to reach Kondapalem village, followed by a short trek to the waterfall.
