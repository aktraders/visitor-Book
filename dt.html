Draksharama Temple


                 

Description

Draksharama Temple is a significant Hindu temple located in Draksharamam, a village in the East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh, India. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it is one of the Pancharama Kshetrams, which are five ancient Shiva temples situated in the region. Here's a comprehensive guide to visiting Draksharama Temple:

How to Reach:

By Air:

The nearest airport is Rajahmundry Airport (about 45 km away). From there, you can take a taxi or bus.

By Train:

The nearest major railway station is Rajahmundry. You can hire a taxi or take a local bus from the station to Draksharamam.

By Road:

Well-connected by road with regular bus services and taxis from nearby cities like Rajahmundry and Kakinada.

Local Attractions:

Rajahmundry:

Known for its beautiful landscapes and the Godavari River.

Kakinada:

A coastal city with attractions like beaches and temples.

Tips for visitors

Dress Code:

Traditional attire is preferred, especially for women. Ensure respectful dress while visiting the temple.

Footwear:

Remove footwear before entering the temple premises.

Offerings:

Common offerings include flowers, fruits, and prasadam. Avoid bringing non-vegetarian items.
