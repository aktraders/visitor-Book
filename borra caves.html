Borra Caves


                 

Description

The Borra Caves are a stunning series of limestone caves located in the Ananthagiri hills of Andhra Pradesh, India. They are known for their impressive stalactites and stalagmites and are believed to be over a million years old. The caves are home to various archaeological artifacts and are a significant tourist attraction.

How to Reach:

By Air:

The nearest airport is Visakhapatnam Airport (about 65 km away). You can hire a taxi or take a bus from there.

By Train:

The nearest major railway station is in Visakhapatnam. Taxis and local buses are available for transport to the caves.

By Road:

Well-connected by road with regular bus services and taxis from nearby cities like Visakhapatnam.

Local Attractions:

Visakhapatnam:

Known for its beautiful beaches, scenic views, and historical sites such as the Submarine Museum.

Ananthagiri Hills:

A picturesque hill station offering trekking and a chance to enjoy nature.
