Thimmamma Marimanu


                 

Key Features

Thimmamma Marimanu Tree:

This ancient banyan tree is famous for its immense size and historical significance, offering a serene spot for visitors.

Video

Scenic Views

Surrounding Landscape:

The area around Thimmamma Marimanu offers picturesque views of nature, perfect for photography and relaxation.

Local Culture

Local festivals celebrate the cultural heritage surrounding this ancient tree, attracting visitors from far and wide.

Activities

Nature Walks:

Enjoy tranquil walks around the area, exploring the lush greenery and serene environment.

Picnicking:

The scenic spots make it an ideal location for picnics with family and friends.

Best Time to Visit

Winter (November to February):

The weather is pleasant, ideal for outdoor activities.

Monsoon (June to September):

The area becomes lush and vibrant, though be cautious of wet paths.
