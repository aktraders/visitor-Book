Location:

District: East Godavari State: Andhra Pradesh Country: India

Nearest Railway Station: Rajahmundry Railway Station (RJY) is the nearest major railway station, approximately 70 kilometers from the beach. It is well connected to various cities across India.

Driving: Chintalamori Beach can be accessed by road from Rajahmundry and nearby towns. The drive offers scenic views and is relatively straightforward. Public Transport: Buses and taxis are available from Rajahmundry and other nearby towns to reach Chintalamori Beach.
