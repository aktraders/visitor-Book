Srikalahasti


                 

Features of Srikalahasti

Sri Kalahasteeswara Temple:

Significance: This is the main attraction, dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is one of the Panchabhootalaya temples, representing the element of Air (Vayu). Architecture: The temple is renowned for its unique architecture and intricate carvings. It features a large tower (Vimana) and is notable for its large shrine and its elaborate sculptures. Special Features: The temple houses a sacred linga known as Kailasa Linga, and it is famous for its Vayu (Air) representation.

Sri Subrahmanya Swamy Temple:

Significance: Dedicated to Lord Subrahmanya (Karthikeya), this temple is located close to the Kalahasteeswara Temple and is an important pilgrimage site. Features: The temple is known for its historical and religious significance and attracts many devotees.

Sri Gnana Saraswati Temple:

Significance: This temple is dedicated to the goddess of knowledge and learning, Saraswati. Location: Situated in the town of Sri Kalahasti, it is often visited by students and scholars seeking blessings for academic success.

Travel and Access

Road:

There are well-maintained roads connecting the two locations, and pilgrims often use buses and taxis.

Bus Services:

The Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation (APSRTC) operates regular bus services from Tirupati to Srikalahasti.

Special Vehicles:

There are also special vehicles arranged by the temple administration for the convenience of pilgrims.

Darshan:

Facilities

Prasadam:

The temple provides prasadam (sacred offerings) such as the famous Srikalahasti pulihora.

Medical Facilities:

Basic medical facilities and emergency services are available for pilgrims.

Seva and Offerings:

There are various seva (services) and offerings that devotees can participate in, including the ritualistic activities and donations to the temple.

Important Considerations

Dress Code:

Pilgrims are expected to adhere to a modest dress code while visiting the temple.

Crowds:

Srikalahasti can be very crowded, especially during peak pilgrimage seasons and festivals. It is advisable to plan the visit accordingly.
