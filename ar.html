Annavaram


                 

Description

By planning ahead and understanding the local customs, you can have a spiritually enriching visit to Annavaram and the Sri Satyanarayana Swamy Temple.

video

Visiting Hours:

Morning:

Typically from 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Evening:

From 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM

Note:

Timings may vary, and special events or festivals may have different schedules.

How to Reach:

By Air:

Rajahmundry Airport (RJA): About 60 km from Annavaram. You can hire a taxi or use local transportation from the airport to the temple.

By Train:

Annavaram Railway Station: Well-connected by trains from major cities. The station is around 5 km from the temple. You can take an auto-rickshaw or taxi from the station.

By Road:

From Rajahmundry: Approximately 60 km by road. Regular bus services and taxis are available. From Visakhapatnam: About 150 km, which takes around 3-4 hours by road. You can hire a taxi or take a bus to Annavaram.

Local Attractions:

Rajahmundry: Offers additional attractions such as the Godavari River, historical sites, and local markets.

A coastal city with attractions like beaches and temples.

Travel Tips:

Dress Code:

Traditional attire is preferred, especially for women. Ensure respectful dress when visiting the temple.

Footwear:

Remove footwear before entering the temple premises.

Offerings:

Common offerings include flowers, fruits, and prasadam. Avoid bringing non-vegetarian items.
