Telineelapuram Birds Sanctuary


                 

Description

Telineelapuram Birds Sanctuary, located in Andhra Pradesh, is a haven for migratory birds and a popular spot for bird watchers. The sanctuary is known for its rich biodiversity and serves as a critical habitat for various bird species.

Video

Nearest Town:

The nearest town is Eluru, approximately 30 kilometers away from the sanctuary.

Accessibility

Transport:

By Road

Telineelapuram is accessible by road from major towns. The sanctuary is well-connected to Eluru and nearby regions.

Best Time to Visit

Season

Winter Season (November to February): This is the best time to visit when migratory birds are in abundance, making it perfect for bird-watching.

Monsoon Season (June to September): While the sanctuary is lush during this time, accessibility may be affected by heavy rainfall.

Attractions and Features

Natural Beauty:

The sanctuary features beautiful landscapes, including wetlands and diverse flora, ideal for nature lovers.

Bird Watching:

Bird watchers can enjoy spotting various migratory species that flock to the sanctuary during winter months.

Tips for Visitors

Carry Binoculars:

For an optimal bird-watching experience, binoculars are highly recommended to observe the diverse species.

Wear Comfortable Clothing:

Dress comfortably and wear suitable footwear for walking around the sanctuary.

Respect Wildlife:

Maintain a respectful distance from wildlife and do not disturb their natural habitat.
