Srivari Steps


                 

Features of Srivari Steps

Scenic Climb:

The Srivari Steps provide a beautiful and serene route for pilgrims, offering stunning views along the way.

Spiritual Significance:

These steps hold great importance for devotees as they represent a sacred journey towards the temple.

Video Journey:

Nearby Temples:

The path includes several smaller temples and shrines, enriching the spiritual experience for visitors.

Travel and Access

By Road:

Well-connected roads lead to the base of the Srivari Steps, making it easily accessible for pilgrims.

Bus Services:

Frequent bus services run from Tirupati to the base of the steps, catering to visitors.

Special Transportation:

During busy seasons, special vehicles are arranged to assist pilgrims in reaching the site.

Timings and Visits

Visiting Hours:

The Srivari Steps are open for pilgrims throughout the day, providing ample time for visitors.

Special Events:

Various religious events and gatherings are held along the steps, adding to the experience.

Facilities

Prasadam:

Local delicacies and prasadam are available at stalls along the route for devotees to enjoy.

Medical Assistance:

Basic medical facilities are available at key points along the steps for any emergencies.

Seva and Offerings:

Visitors can engage in various seva opportunities and offerings during their visit.

Important Considerations

Dress Code:

A modest dress code is recommended to respect the sanctity of the area.

Crowds:

Expect large crowds during festivals; it's advisable to plan visits during off-peak times.
