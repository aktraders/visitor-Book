Sri Naganathaswami Temple


                 

Description

Sri ranaganathaswami Temple, located in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh, is a revered temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is known for its stunning architecture and spiritual significance, attracting numerous devotees.

History:

This ancient temple has a rich history, believed to have been constructed centuries ago, and is associated with various legends of Lord Shiva, making it a vital pilgrimage site.

Deity:

The temple is dedicated to Lord Naganathaswami, an incarnation of Lord Shiva, worshipped here in the form of a Linga.

Architectural Features

Style:

The temple showcases traditional Dravidian architecture, with intricately carved pillars and a majestic tower (Shikhara) that adds to its beauty.

Main Shrine:

The main shrine houses the Linga of Lord Naganathaswami, drawing in numerous pilgrims for worship and rituals.

Intricate Carvings:

The temple is famous for its detailed carvings that depict various deities and mythological stories from Hindu scriptures.

Tips for Visitors

Comfortable Clothing:

Wear modest clothing that covers shoulders and knees to show respect in the temple.
