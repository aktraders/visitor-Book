Flora and Fauna:

The region is rich in biodiversity, including a variety of trees, plants, and bird species.

Best Time to Visit

Winter (October to February):

Ideal time to visit due to cool and pleasant weather.

Summer (March to June)

Warm but still cooler than the plains. Early morning or late evening visits are preferable.

Activities

Picnicking:

Ideal for picnics amidst natural surroundings. Many spots offer facilities for a relaxing day out.

Trekking and Nature Walks:

There are various trails suitable for trekking and nature walks, allowing visitors to explore the lush greenery and wildlife.

Photography:

The scenic beauty makes it a great location for photography, especially at sunrise and sunset.

How to reach

By Road

Well-connected by road. You can hire a taxi or use local buses from Chittoor or Tirupati.

By Train:

The nearest major railway station is in Chittoor. From there, you can take a taxi or local bus.
