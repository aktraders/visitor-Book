Hamsaladeevi


                 

Description

Hamsaladeevi is a picturesque coastal village located in Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh. Known for its serene beaches and vibrant culture, it attracts visitors looking for both relaxation and adventure. The village is also famous for its historical significance and beautiful landscapes.

Video

Attractions:

Beaches:

The beaches of Hamsaladeevi offer stunning views and are perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying beach activities.

Visiting Hours

General:

The beaches are accessible at all hours, but it's advisable to visit during the day for safety and enjoyment.

How to Reach:

By Air:

The nearest airport is Vijayawada Airport (about 60 km away). From there, you can take a taxi or bus.

By Train:

The nearest major railway station is Machilipatnam. You can hire a taxi or take a local bus to reach Hamsaladeevi.

By Road:

Well-connected by road, with regular bus services and taxis from nearby cities like Vijayawada and Machilipatnam.

Tips for Visitors

Dress Code:

Comfortable and casual attire is recommended, especially if you plan to spend time on the beach.

Footwear:

Wear comfortable footwear, as you may walk on sandy and uneven surfaces.

Safety:

Always follow safety guidelines provided by local authorities, especially when swimming or participating in water activities.
