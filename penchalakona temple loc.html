Location

Penchalakona Temple, Penchalakona Village, Brahmanapalle Mandal, Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh, India.

By Road: Penchalakona Temple is approximately 30 kilometers from Nellore city. You can reach it by taking the NH16 (National Highway 16) and then following the local roads to the temple. The roads are well-maintained and accessible for cars and buses.

By Train: The nearest major railway station is Nellore Railway Station. From there, you can take a taxi or a local bus to reach the temple.
