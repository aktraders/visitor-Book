Location:

District: Srikakulam, State: Andhra Pradesh

Nearest Town: Srikakulam Town: About 20 km away (approximately 30 minutes by road).
Nearby City: Visakhapatnam: Approximately 100 km away (around 2 to 2.5 hours by road).

Accessibility
By Road: Salihundam is accessible via well-maintained roads from Srikakulam. It can be reached by car or local transport. Public Transport:
Local buses and auto-rickshaws are available for traveling to the site. Rail:
The nearest railway station is in Srikakulam, from which you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Salihundam.
