Location: Borra Caves
District: Visakhapatnam
State: Andhra Pradesh
Nearest Town: Araku Valley (approximately 37 kilometers away)

Nearest Railway Station: Visakhapatnam Railway Station, which is well connected to major cities like Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kolkata. From the railway station, you can take a taxi or local transport to reach the caves.
From Visakhapatnam City To Borra Caves: Take NH 30 towards Araku Valley. The caves are about 90 kilometers from the city center and are accessible by road. It typically takes around 2-3 hours to reach.

Local Travel: Taxis and local buses are available for travel to Borra Caves from Visakhapatnam and Araku Valley. The route is scenic, passing through beautiful landscapes.
