Description

Puttaparthi Lake, also known as the Chitravathi River or Chitravathi Reservoir, is a significant natural feature near Puttaparthi in Andhra Pradesh, India. Here’s a detailed guide for visiting and exploring Puttaparthi Lake:

video

Attractions

Natural beauty

The lake is surrounded by hills and lush greenery, making it an ideal spot for nature enthusiasts.

Bird Watching:

The area around the lake is a good spot for bird watching, with various local bird species present.

Sunset Views:

The lake provides beautiful sunset views, offering a serene environment for evening visits.

Visitor Information

Timings:

General Access: The lake is open throughout the day. However, visiting during daylight hours is recommended. Best Time to Visit: Early morning or late afternoon for pleasant weather and better views.

Entry Fee:

Typically, there is no entry fee for visiting the lake. However, it’s good to check locally for any specific regulations.

Things to Do

Picnicking:

Enjoy a picnic by the lake with family or friends. Carry food and drinks as facilities may be limited.

Photography:

Capture the natural beauty and serene landscapes of the lake.

Leisure Walks:

Take a leisurely walk around the lake to enjoy the scenic views and peaceful environment.

Travel Tips

Dress Code

Wear comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for walking.

Carry Water and Snacks:

Bring water, snacks, and sunscreen, especially if you plan to spend extended time outdoors.

Photography:

Photography is generally prohibited
