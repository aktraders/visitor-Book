Location:

Full Name: Thimmamma Marimanu, District: Ananthapuramu, State: Andhra Pradesh, India, Nearest Town: Kadiri (about 20 km away), Nearby Villages: Thimmamma Marimanu is situated near the village of the same name, which is a small and serene locale.

From Kadiri: Take the Kadiri-Banavasi Road, which leads to Thimmamma Marimanu. The drive takes about 30 minutes.

From Ananthapur: Approximately 80 km away, you can take NH 44 (formerly NH 7) to reach Kadiri and then follow local roads.
