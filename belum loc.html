Location:

District: Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, Nearest Town: Kolimigundla

Distance from Kurnool: About 100 kilometers (62 miles), Accessibility: The caves are about 3 kilometers from the village of Belum.

To reach the caves, you can drive from Kurnool or take public transport to Kolimigundla and then hire a local vehicle to the caves. The nearest major city with a railway station is Kurnool, and the nearest airport is in Hyderabad, around 220 kilometers away.
